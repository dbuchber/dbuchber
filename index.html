Paperless Digital Office

Built a fully paperless office using paperless-ngx, with automated invoice pre-accounting via local LLMs running on Ollama. Document classification and data extraction handled through n8n workflows.

Question Can local, self-hosted LLMs reliably extract and pre-account structured invoice data without any cloud dependency?
Methods LLM-based extraction via Ollama, n8n workflow orchestration, paperless-ngx document management, Docker-based self-hosting.

Research

Bachelor Thesis.

Graph-grounded question answering — at WU Vienna.

Title
Reducing Hallucinations in LLM-Based Question Answering through Hybrid Multi-Agent Knowledge Graph Traversal
In Progress · 2026

LLMs can sound confident and be completely wrong. This thesis asks whether grounding answers in a large-scale, human-curated knowledge graph — traversed in parallel by multiple agents — can change that. Evaluated in a domain where being wrong is not just a low score.

Research Focus

  • Hybrid graph traversal with parallel sub-agents
  • Hallucination reduction & answer faithfulness
  • Human-curated vs. LLM-extracted knowledge graphs

Technologies & Methods

  • Neo4j · Wikidata · SPARQL
  • LangChain · LlamaIndex
  • Ollama · vllm · open-weight LLMs
  • Python · MLflow
